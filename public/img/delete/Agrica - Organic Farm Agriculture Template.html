Overview

Keralas agriculture sector has access to world-class innovations from AI-powered precision farming and climate-resilient practices to advanced post-harvest and supply-chain technologies. However, the real challenge today is not the lack of innovation, but the lack of large-scale adoption at the farm level.

AgriNext Summit 2026 is conceived as a high-impact, implementation-driven summit to bridge this critical gap by directly connecting Farmers, FPOs/FPCs, and Agri-Startups, and enabling real deployments on the ground.

AgriNext under KERA

Kerala Startup Mission is implementing the AgriNext Project under the KERA Scheme, in partnership with the Department of Agriculture, Government of Kerala, and supported by the World Bank.

Enabling farmer access to proven, affordable, and scalable technologies

Creating clear deployment and procurement pathways for agri-startups

Strengthening FPOs and FPCs as adoption and scaling anchors

Vision: A Farmer-Centric Adoption Ecosystem

To establish Kerala as the national benchmark for accelerated, farmer-first agri-innovation deployment.
AgriNext goes beyond discussion and ideation it is designed to deliver tangible outcomes, including pilots, partnerships, procurement, and measurable impact across agricultural districts.

Objectives

Match & Deploy

Facilitate structured matchmaking between Farmers/FPOs/FPCs and validated Agri-Startups, leading to signed pilot MoUs, adoption agreements, and procurement pathways.

ProblemSolution Mapping

Identify district-specific and crop-specific agricultural challenges and map them to market-ready, deployable technology solutions.

Empower FPO & FPC Anchors

Position FPOs and FPCs as the primary vehicles for technology assimilation, collective adoption, and scale across farmer clusters.

Scalable Model Creation

Develop a replicable, high-efficiency framework for sustained, state-wide agri-innovation deployment under the KERA ecosystem.
